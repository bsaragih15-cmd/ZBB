MPI Budget Cockpit
Zero-based budgeting, benchmarked to best. A cost cockpit that benchmarks controllable O&M across every asset down to PO level (L5) — pinpointing inefficiencies and surfacing savings in one view.
Key Features
1
Asset Cost Scorecard
A card per asset: cost intensity ($/kW-yr), total controllable spend, and whether it leads the fleet or has money at stake.
2
3-Way Benchmark Matrix
Every cost line compared across assets and against best-in-fleet, the external market, and like-for-like (size-normalized) — with an accountable owner named on every line.
3
Savings Simulator
Dial how aggressively to close the gap and see the efficiency prize benchmarked three ways at once — best plant, external market, and the stretch frontier.
4
Prioritized Recommendations
Automatically ranks the biggest savings opportunities and pre-drafts a challenge memo for each cost owner — every figure cited, the decision yours. (AI-assisted, optional.)
5
Drill-Down Workflow
Trace any gap from fleet level to its root, L3 to L5 per asset, then straight into a board-ready challenge pack.
◇
Analyst-grade & auditable
Every figure traces to its source line; benchmarks span best-in-fleet, the external market, and like-for-like — your numbers, your methodology.
